PB&J

  • Time: 2 mins
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 4
Jam 1
Peanut-Butter 1

Step 1: Heat the bread.
Step 2: Apply jam on one side.
Step 3: Apply peanut-butter on other side.

Source: Pinch-of-Yum

Egg Salad

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3
Onion 1/2
Tomato 1
Lemon 1

Step 1: Boil the eggs and de-shell them.
Step 2: Cut the eggs in bite sized pieces.
Step 3: Chop the vegetables and add to eggs.
Step 4: Finally add lemon and serve.

Source: Salad-Kings

Cappucino

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Coffee 1 tbsp
Water 250 ml
Ice-Cubes 4

Step 1: Add Coffee to glass.
Step 2: Tilt glass and add water slowly from a height.
Step 3: Add ice cubes.

Source: Cofee-House
